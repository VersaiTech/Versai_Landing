ETHEREUM TOKEN DEVELOPMENT SERVICES

Token Development

Crypto tokens are crypto coins that do not have their own blockchain, and they are built on an existing blockchain network like Ethereum. In other words, they are digital assets that have different features and functionalities than altcoins and bitcoins. They can represent a wide range of assets - both digital and physical.

These tokens are created using standard templates of existing blockchains, and then they are distributed by ICOs and STOs in a digital coin market where they can represent a specific fungible or tradable asset. That is why Startups and entrepreneurs prefer them to raise funds on crowdfunding platforms. Talking about their applications, they act as a tool or medium for secure transactions, creating decentralized apps, and executing smart contracts.

Ethereum is an open-source blockchain network. Our developers at Versai Technology can use the open-source Ethereum network to build crypto tokens backed by self-executing code. This code is then used to perform and manage the different transactions on the blockchain.
